About

Welcome to the Belle Urban System mobile application. The purpose of the application is to provide navigation around Racine using the Belle Urban buses. By using the Near Me feature, you can get a list of the stops near you.

Fares:

  • Adult – 2.00
  • Youth Fare – 2.00
  • Children (0-5) Free when accompanied by an adult
  • Senior Citizen (65 and over) 1.00
  • Disabled – 1.00
  • Valid Medicare Card Holder Picture – 1.00
  • Dial - A - Ride (DART) – 4.00
